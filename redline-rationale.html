Why rationale, not just diff

Most redline tools detect and classify edits — useful, but they stop at "this sentence changed." The judgment a lawyer adds is the part that matters: a 30-day cure period cut to 5 days isn't just a number change, it's a shift of leverage toward whoever can now terminate faster. This tool surfaces that — what each change does to each side's position — with every reading flagged for a human to confirm. It isn't limited to the examples above; paste any clause from any agreement.
